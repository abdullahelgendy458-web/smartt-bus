Smart Bus

INTRODUCTION

Public transportation, especially buses, suffers from several social problems, the most prominent of which is harassment in its various forms, whether verbal or physical. This issue is aggravated by severe crowding, limited space, and the lack of supervision inside the bus, which creates an environment that allows inappropriate behavior to occur. Victims often hesitate to report such incidents due to embarrassment or fear of social judgment, which leads to these behaviors repeating without any deterrence. In addition to that, other problems also occur, such as: Fights and conflicts between passengers Theft or verbal/physical disturbances Lack of personal privacy Over time, these issues negatively affect passengers’ sense of safety and reduce the use of public transportation. This problem requires a smart and modern solution that can enhance safety and reduce harmful behaviors inside the bus.
