Camera Module Circuit

Components:

  • ESP32-CAM module or Raspberry Pi Camera module
  • 2. MicroSD card (for ESP32-CAM)
  • 3. Power supply (5V)
  • 4. Antenna (if required)
  • 5. Jumper wires

Function:

Captures images or video and can stream them or store them depending on the module used.

SMS Communication Circuit (SIM8800L)

Components:

  • SIM8800L cellular module
  • 2. Microcontroller (UART communication/)
  • 3. External antenna
  • 4. Stable power source (3.7–4.2V, 2A peak/)
  • 5. Jumper wires / level shifter if needed

Function:

Captures images or video and can stream them or store them depending on the module used.

Passenger Counter Circuit (7-Segment + IR Sensors)

Components:

  • IR emitter and IR receiver pair (two sets for in/out)
  • 2. Microcontrolle
  • 3. 7-segment display (single or multi-digit)
  • 4.Display driver (e.g., 74HC595 / TM1637 / MAX7219)
  • 5. Resistors and jumper wires

Function:

Counts people entering and exiting using IR beams, then displays the number on the 7-segment

Pulse & Oxygen Monitoring Circuit (MAX30102)

Components:

  • MAX30102 pulse oximeter sensor
  • 2. Microcontroller with I²C support
  • 3. SDA, SCL, VCC (3.3V), GND connections
  • 4. Jumper wires

Function:

Captures images or video and can stream them or store them depending on the module used.

Temperature Measurement Circuit (DS18B20)

Components:

  • DS18B20 temperature senso
  • 2. 4.7kΩ pull-up resistor (DATA → VCC)
  • 3. Microcontroller (Arduino / ESP32)
  • 4. umper wires and power supply
  • 5. (Optional) OLED or LCD display

Function:

Measures digital temperature and sends it to the microcontroller through the 1-Wire communication line.
